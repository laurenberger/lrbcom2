Lauren Berger

After graduating from Rochester Institute of Technology with a BS in Psychology, I joined the PhD program in Educational Neuroscience at Gallaudet University. Here, I am a resident of Dr. Clifton Langdon's Language and Educational Neuroscience laboratory, and a Graduate Research Fellow with the National Science Foundation. 
My primary interests explore the plasticity and resilience of the neural substrates supporting the language network. I study populations with variations in sensory and language experiences. Studying a combination of the two will inform us of the neural underpinnings of language processing systems.
